The goal is to create a voice chatbot that can be used to book an appointment with a service provider, such as a hairdresser, car mechanic, personal customer service or doctor. In this third block we don't tie you to the tools you use, you can use a colab, your own machine, whatever suits you best. We suggest solutions for each week, but you can also use something else.

- The dialogue should ask for the most important information to be confirmed. User identification (name, email, phone number or other method), time, treatment or service chosen, other extra information requested (e.g. emotional state). The results should be available in a json format.
- The system should work in English and/or Hungarian.
