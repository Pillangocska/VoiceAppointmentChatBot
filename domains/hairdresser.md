# Studio Lumen Hair Salon — Service Information

## Opening hours

- Tuesday to Friday: 10:00 – 20:00
- Saturday: 09:00 – 18:00
- Sunday and Monday: closed

The salon is closed for two weeks every August; exact dates are posted
on the front door and on Instagram each year.

## Booking policy

- Online booking opens four weeks in advance via studiolumen.hu/book.
- A phone confirmation is sent the day before the appointment. Clients
  who do not confirm by 18:00 the previous day risk losing the slot.
- Late cancellations (under 24 hours) are charged 50% of the service
  price. No-shows are charged in full and must prepay the next visit.
- Colour services require a quick patch test 48 hours in advance for
  first-time clients; this can be done in the salon in five minutes.

## Payment

Cash, all major cards, Apple Pay and Google Pay accepted. SZÉP card is
not accepted. A 10% service charge is added for groups of three or more.
