# Paws & Whiskers Veterinary Clinic — Service Information

## Opening hours

- Monday to Friday: 08:00 – 19:00
- Saturday: 09:00 – 14:00
- Sunday: closed (emergency line only)

The clinic is closed on Hungarian public holidays. The on-call emergency
number is +36 1 555 0199 and is staffed 24/7.

## Address and contact

Paws & Whiskers, Bartók Béla út 42, 1114 Budapest. Phone: +36 1 555 0100.
Free street parking after 18:00; a paid garage is two doors down.

## Species we treat

We treat dogs, cats, rabbits, ferrets, guinea pigs, hamsters, and most
small caged birds. We do **not** treat reptiles, fish, or exotic mammals
such as sugar gliders — for those we refer clients to ExoticVet Budapest.

## Booking policy

- We book consultations in 20-minute slots and surgical procedures in
  the morning so the animal can be observed during the day.
- New patients should arrive 10 minutes early to fill in a registration
  form. Bringing prior medical records speeds this up.
- Vaccination appointments require the pet to be in good health on the
  day; if the animal is unwell we will reschedule at no charge.
- We accept cash, all major cards, and SZÉP card (Egészségpénztár) for
  preventive services.

## Insurance and payment

We have direct billing arrangements with K&H Pet Insurance and Generali
Pet. For other insurers we issue a detailed invoice the owner can submit
for reimbursement.
